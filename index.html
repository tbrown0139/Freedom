A Nation Worth Defending

At Knovon, our core culture is the United States of America — a nation and a history we are proud of. We believe the Constitution is not merely a document from the past. It is the living charter that guarantees fundamental freedom to every American.

The Bill of Rights secures the liberties that make self-government possible: freedom of speech and religion, the right to bear arms, protection from unreasonable search and seizure, due process, and the reservation of power to the states and the people.

These rights belong to you. They were fought for, written into law, and passed down through generations. Our mission is to make sure you know them, value them, and are ready to defend them.

The Bill of Rights

Ratified in 1791, the first ten amendments establish the essential protections every citizen must know:

  • Freedom of speech, religion, press, and assembly
  • Right to keep and bear arms
  • Protection against quartering of soldiers
  • Freedom from unreasonable searches and seizures
  • Rights of the accused in criminal cases
  • Right to trial by jury and protection from excessive punishment
  • Recognition of unenumerated rights retained by the people
  • Limits on federal power; powers reserved to states and people

How Government Is Limited

The Constitution was designed to restrain government, not the people:

  • Separation of powers among three branches
  • Checks and balances to prevent tyranny
  • Federalism dividing authority between nation and states
  • Enumerated powers — government may only do what the Constitution permits
  • Due process of law before life, liberty, or property may be taken

Your Civic Duty

Preserving the republic requires active citizens, not passive spectators:

  • Read and study the Constitution and its amendments
  • Follow legislation that affects your rights
  • Vote in every election — local, state, and federal
  • Speak up when rights are threatened
  • Teach the next generation what freedom costs

Stand Guard Over Liberty

The Constitution will not defend itself. Vigilant citizens must.

Resist Government Overreach

Big government grows at the expense of individual liberty. When federal power expands beyond constitutional limits, your rights are the first casualty. Know the Tenth Amendment. Hold elected officials accountable. Demand that government stay within its proper bounds.

Guard Against Foreign Influence

America's sovereignty and self-determination must not be compromised by foreign interests, undue influence, or agendas that undermine our constitutional order. A free nation requires citizens who recognize threats to independence and refuse to yield control of their government.

Defend the Rights of the People

Your freedoms of speech, religion, assembly, and self-defense exist precisely so that power cannot silence dissent or disarm the citizenry. Exercise your rights. Defend them for your neighbors. Liberty shared is liberty preserved.

Why We Must Act Now

History teaches that free governments are fragile. There will come a time when the Constitution and the government of the United States hang by a brittle thread — ready to fall into other hands unless the people rise to defend it.

We do not wait for that day unprepared. Through education, civic engagement, and unwavering commitment to constitutional principles, Knovon works to ensure that thread holds strong for generations to come.

  • Promote understanding of the Constitution and Bill of Rights
  • Defend individual liberties against government overreach
  • Resist foreign influence on American sovereignty
  • Equip citizens to participate in preserving the republic
